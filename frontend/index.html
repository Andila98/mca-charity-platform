Who We Are

Ajelo Foundation is an organisation dedicated to bridging the gap in healthcare access. We believe that quality medical care is a basic human right, not a privilege. Every day, we work tirelessly to fund treatments,and ongoing care for those facing financial barriers — bringing hope and healing to families in need.
