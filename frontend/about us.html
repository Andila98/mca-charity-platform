Our Mission & Vision

We believe that quality healthcare is a fundamental human right, not a privilege. Ajelo Foundation exists to bridge the gap in medical accessibility by providing direct financial support to individuals and families who lack the resources for critical medical care.

Our vision is a future where financial hardship never stands between a person and life-saving medical treatment. Through strategic partnerships, community engagement, and transparent operations, we transform compassion into action.

Namdala Ajelo William

Founder & Executive Director

Born and raised in Kalait, Angurai North, within Busia County, Kenya, Namdala Ajelo Williams carries the spirit of his roots into every endeavor. His journey from the vibrant landscapes of Busia to the forefront of social impact has been driven by a singular belief: that every community, no matter how remote, deserves a seat at the table of global progress.

As the founder of Ajelo Foundation,Namdala focuses his leadership on the Intersection of three pivitol pillars:

  • Transformation
  • Technology
  • Globalization

Transformation: Namdala is dedicated to holistic community growth. He believes that true change isn't just about temporary aid, but about shifting mindsets and building sustainable systems that empower individuals to become authors of their own futures.

Technology: Recognizing the digital divide as one of the greatest challenges of our time, Namdala champions the use of technology as a bridge. He works to bring digital literacy and innovative tools to underserved regions, ensuring that "home" is never a barrier to opportunity.

Our Journey

2018

Foundation Established

Ajelo Foundation officially registered as an NGO in Kenya, starting with grassroots medical outreach programs in Nairobi's underserved communities.

2019

First 100 Lives Impacted

Through partnerships with local hospitals, we facilitated emergency care and treatment for our first 100 patients in critical need.

2020

Digital Platform Launch

Launched our online donation platform and volunteer management system to scale impact and improve transparency for our donors.

2021

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to Mombasa, Nakuru, and Kisumu. Founder recognized as "Young Social Entrepreneur of the Year" by East Africa Social Impact Awards.

2022

KES 20M Milestone

Reached our first major milestone facilitating KES 20 million in medical assistance. Launched community health education initiative.

2024

KES 50M+ & Beyond

Passed KES 50 million in total medical assistance. Now supporting 1,000+ patients. Planning for international expansion and research initiatives.
